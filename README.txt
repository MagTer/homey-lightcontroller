Lighting Scheduler automates your Homey Pro lights through four daily phases: Night, Morning, Day and Evening. Instead of configuring every lamp individually, you group devices into roles and define how each role should behave during every phase. The app then transitions your lights automatically based on time, sunrise and sunset, or indoor lux levels.

You can skip wake-up lights when it is already bright, dim lights smoothly as daylight fades, and the app respects manual overrides so it never fights your own adjustments. Lux sensors, phase schedules, roles and Flow cards are all configured from the app settings.
