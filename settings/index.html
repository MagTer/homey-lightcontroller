Light Controller

Automate your lights through four daily phases

What is a role?

A role is a group of devices that should behave the same way during each phase of the day. Instead of configuring every lamp individually, you assign lamps to a role and control them together.

💡 Example: create a "Living Room" role with your sofa lamp and ceiling light, then set them to dim to 30% in the EVENING and turn off at NIGHT.
The four phases

Your lights transition automatically between these phases based on time, sunrise/sunset, or light level.

NIGHT — All off MORNING — Gentle wake-up DAY — Natural light EVENING — Cozy dim

Select lux sensors to enable light-aware transitions and twilight dimming.
